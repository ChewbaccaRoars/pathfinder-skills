# Pathfinder Intake Template

> Use this during Week 1 of a tour. Send the form questions to the team in advance, then use the interview guide for 1:1 conversations with the team lead and key members.

---

## 1:1 Interview Guide (for conversations with team lead and key members)

*Use this as a conversation guide, not a rigid script. Let the conversation flow naturally.*

### Opening (2 min)
- "I'm here to learn how your team works and find opportunities to save time or reduce friction. I'm not auditing anything — I'm looking for things I can help build."
- "Walk me through a typical Monday. What's the first thing you do when you sit down?"

### Daily Work (10 min)
- "What does a typical day look like for you?"
- "What takes up most of your time?"
- "What's the most boring part of your job?" *(people are honest about this one)*
- "What do you do that feels like it should be automatic but isn't?"
- "Are there things you do that require switching between multiple tools or systems?"

### Information Flow (5 min)
- "Where does your data come from? Who sends it to you?"
- "What do you do with it once you have it?"
- "Who do you send your output to? In what format?"
- "Is there a lot of copy-pasting between systems?"

### Pain Points (5 min)
- "What breaks? What causes fire drills?"
- "What's the thing that, when it goes wrong, costs the most time to fix?"
- "If you had a magic wand, what would you change about how your team works?"
- "What's something that used to be fine but doesn't scale anymore?"

### Tribal Knowledge (3 min)
- "Is there anything that only one person on the team knows how to do?"
- "What would happen if that person was out for a week?"
- "Are there any processes that aren't documented anywhere?"

### Closing (2 min)
- "Based on what we've talked about, I'm going to look for the highest-impact thing I can build in the next few weeks."
- "I'll share what I find with you before building anything."
- "Who else on the team should I talk to?"
