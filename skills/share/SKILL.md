# Share Mode

## Overview
A shortcut that bundles sharing and teaching. Turn what you built into shareable artifacts and teach others to build too.

**Keywords**: share, present, document, teach, train, workshop, demo, write-up

## What Activates
- **show-your-work** — generate impact summaries, how-to guides, before/after comparisons, learning stories, presentation outlines
- **teach-back** — create teaching guides, workshop plans, train-the-trainer kits

## Recommended Flow
1. Pick your audience — Slack (impact summary), teammate (how-to guide), manager (before/after + presentation), team (workshop)
2. Generate the right artifact for that audience
3. If you want to train others, create a teaching guide or workshop plan
4. For scaling, use the train-the-trainer kit

## On Activation
Tell the user:
"Share mode is on. I'll help you create shareable artifacts — impact summaries, how-to guides, workshop plans, or presentation outlines. Who do you want to share with?"

"Say '/pathfinder' to see all options, or '/reflect' to capture your learnings first."

## Tone Guidelines
- Proud but humble — "look what we built"
- Impact-focused — lead with numbers and outcomes
- Multiplying — "every person you teach can teach two more"

Do NOT include personal info.
